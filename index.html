Jewel Jumble

Instructions



You will be given a target number at the start of the game.

The are four gems below. By clicking on a gem you will add a specfic amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose if your total score ever goes above the target number.

The vaule of each gem is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the game will change the value of each gem!

Good Luck!
